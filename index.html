The cosmos is infinite, the brain is limitless & the science is unbounded.
So, lets aspire to reach the unreachable.

I have always been fascinated by anything that positively impacts people's lives, this have directed me to acquire a BSc in Mechatronics & Industrial Robotics as I have seen some great technologies like autonomous cars & embedded systems are adding a great value to the community. Meanwhile, the unprecedented impact that Machine Learning has shwon accross all disciplines has stimulated me to shift to this field & embark on a journey of exploration into this deep multidsiplinary spectrum.
And as I always wanted to be an astronaut where I can explore the infinite universe, Machine Learning has made this possible & more of a reality with all its potentials & capabilities where anyone can explore the whole world through the lenses of the massive amounts of available data. With my ongoing MSc in Data Science, I'm hoping the journey unfolds huge successes & contributions to the field & positive influence to people & their lives.

Get ready for some thrill and let's go!
